Hi there! My name is Jason and welcome to my learning journal.

After several months in the Frontend Develoer Career Path, I've developed and deployed several solo projects.

How I stay commited to learning

I like to thik of myself as a lifelong learner. I used to spend hours and hours learning, then try to create simple projects using what I learned or work new techniques into existing projects.

While that was fun, I felt like it would be helpful to share what I was learning and most things about my journey with the world.

How I got started

I started simple and gradually grew my learning journal site. I would take notes about what I was learning. After each learning session, I'd use my notes to not only reflect on what I learned but also write short summaries of what I learned using my own words.

That helped me retain what I was learning, and I realized that posting my learning summaries was also helping others learn and stay motivated.
